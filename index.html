Our Mission

We believe genuine human connection is a basic universal need.

Our Community Values

  • We keep our hearts open, and genuinely care about all of our friends in the platform.

  • Absolutely no judging, bullying, or harassment of any kind.

  • Genuinely believe that all of us humans are in this journey called life together, and deserve the support of each other.
